Som Tam

(or Spicy Papaya Salad)

Green papaya salad has many variations throughout Thailand, but the origianl style

comes from the northeastern region of Issan.

Ingredients

  • 5 Cherry Tomatoes
  • 2 chili peppers
  • 1 1/2 tablespoons fish sauce
  • 1 clove raw garlic
  • 6 raw green beans
  • 2 cups shredded green papaya
  • 3/4 lime juice
  • 1 1/2 tablespoons Palm Sugar
  • 1 teaspoon dried shrimp (if you like it really fishy!)
  • 2 tablespoons toasted peanuts (optional)

Instructions

  1. Smash a clove of garlic in a large sturdy bowl or mortar
  2. Add green beans and halved cherry tomatoes, and continue mashing to brring the juicews out of the beans and tomatoes
  3. Add chilis, peanuts and shrimp (if your using them), and sugar, and continue mashing
  4. Finish the main ingredients by adding the shredded green papaya, mixing everything well
  5. Fianlly add the fish sauce and lime juice to taste, the final dish should have a sweet, sour, and savory taste
